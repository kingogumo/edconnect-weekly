About Ogumo Prince O.

Personal Information

Ogumo Prince Oyeintonbra popularly known as Gumieez (born December 27) is a fitness enthusiast and trainer. He hails from Bayelsa State, the southern part of Nigeria. When he isn't working out, he is either learning a new skill, reading educationals, listening to music or playing video games. He loves to solve problems, oversee projects, and ensure organisation in every platform he finds himself. He gained admission into covenant university in 2011, where he spent 4 years but was unable to graduate due to financial and personal reasons. In 2017, he applied into Babcock University as a transfer student and was granted admission to study Computer Science (Information Systems). Over these periods he worked as an intern at Aero Contractors of Nigeria (2015) and Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation (2019) providing I.T support services accross all departments of these organisations respectively. He also pursued a career in event security working as a protocol officer with PETREZ GUARDZ and DEXON. He is currently undergoing training, mostly self-taught to develop I.T skills that'll be valuable to him in securing a well paying job and as well start his company as an I.T services solutions and consultancy firm.
